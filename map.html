The Life and Ministry of Jesus

Welcome to this interactive map exploring the major locations from Jesus' life and ministry as recorded in the New Testament.

How to Use This Map:

  • Each numbered marker represents a significant location in Jesus' life.
  • Different colors represent different phases of his ministry.
  • Hover over any marker to see the location name.
  • Click on any marker to learn about what happened at that location.
  • Use the zoom controls to explore the geography of the Holy Land.

Color Key:

Birth & Early Life
Baptism & Temptation
Ministry in Galilee
Ministry in Judea
Passion Week
Resurrection & Ascension

The map includes 23 key locations, covering the entire scope of Jesus' life from his birth in Bethlehem to his ascension from the Mount of Olives.

Each location information window includes:

  • A detailed description of what happened there
  • Space for multimedia content
  • Biblical references for further study
